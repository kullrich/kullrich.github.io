Source code and useful scripts for bioinformatic projects.

CRBHits

CRBHits is a reimplementation of the Conditional Reciprocal Best Hit algorithm crb-blast in R. It covers all necessary steps from CRBHit pair calculation to Codon Alignments and Ka/Ks.

Training

Notes and some examples how to use and for population genomics and phylogeny.

RootDetection

RootDetection is an automated tool for evaluating photographs of plant roots. It detects single strand roots, traces their paths and measures the resulting lengths - completely automatic..
